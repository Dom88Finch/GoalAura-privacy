## How the App Works

GoalAura connects to a backend API hosted on **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** for 
the sole purpose of fetching football-related data required for the App's 
functionality.

* All requests are **read-only**
* No personal data is sent to the server
* Requests are not linked to individual users or devices
* No identifiers or tracking information are transmitted

Any preferences or settings within the App are stored **locally on your 
device only**. Deleting the App will remove all locally stored data.

---

## Local Data Caching

To improve performance and provide offline access, the App temporarily 
caches football-related data on your device. This cached data:

* Contains only football match data and statistics
* Does not include any personal information
* Is automatically refreshed periodically
* Can be cleared manually through the App's settings
* Is removed entirely when the App is deleted

## Subscriptions and Payments

The App offers optional in-app subscriptions managed entirely through 
Apple's App Store. 

* All payments are processed by **Apple** through your Apple ID
* We do **not** collect, store, or have access to your payment information
* Subscription management (including cancellation) is handled through 
  your Apple ID account settings
* We use **RevenueCat** as a subscription management platform to verify 
  subscription status. RevenueCat processes an anonymous app-specific 
  identifier and does not receive any personal information

For more information about how Apple handles your payment data, please 
refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

For more information about how RevenueCat handles data, please refer to 
[RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/).
